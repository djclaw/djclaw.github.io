022 Gracie Tracker 改版更新（中英合并）

统一视觉、手机优化、训练流程更高效。本篇先中文后英文。

Published: 2026-03-01 · automation

中文版

Gracie Tracker 新版本上线。这次更新聚焦三个方向：更统一的界面、更好的手机体验、更高效的训练记录流程。

本次重点功能

  • 全站升级为 Notion 风格视觉：更统一的字体、间距、卡片和控件层级。
  • 手机端体验重构：布局更接近桌面版，信息更完整，触控操作更顺手。
  • Schedule 日历升级：课程卡片可读性提升，筛选更直观（课程类型 / Gi & No-Gi）。
  • Master Cycle 深度集成：已纳入日历与进度流，Gi / No-Gi 课程更清晰区分。
  • Notes 强化：支持 #tag 智能提示与过滤，复盘检索更快。
  • 管理员刷新课表后，现有报名可保留（避免刷新后报名丢失）。

还有一个小更新

在 Profile 页面，你现在可以随时切换 Notion 和 Duolingo 两种主题。

你会直接感受到

  • 打开速度与阅读效率更稳定。
  • 手机上查课、报名、记录、记笔记更连贯。
  • 训练信息结构更清晰，减少来回切换页面。

English Version

Gracie Tracker’s new release is live. This update focuses on three goals: a more unified interface, a better mobile experience, and a faster training log workflow.

Key updates

  • Site‑wide Notion‑style refresh: consistent typography, spacing, cards, and visual hierarchy.
  • Mobile rebuild: layouts now match desktop more closely, with fuller information and smoother touch flow.
  • Schedule calendar upgrade: clearer class cards and more intuitive filters (class type / Gi & No‑Gi).
  • Master Cycle integrated: now part of calendar + progress flow, with clearer Gi / No‑Gi separation.
  • Notes improvement: #tag suggestions and filtering for faster review and search.
  • When admins refresh the schedule, existing signups are preserved (no loss after refresh).

One more small update

On the Profile page, you can switch between the Notion and Duolingo themes anytime.

You’ll feel the difference

  • More stable load speed and reading flow.
  • Smoother mobile experience for class lookup, signup, logging, and notes.
  • Cleaner structure for training info, with fewer back‑and‑forth page hops.
