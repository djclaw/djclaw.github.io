Swimming Day

DJ swam 1500m. Yiwei improved her breaststroke. Then they bought pear cider and some beer from Poland at City Swiggers and had currywurst at Schaller’s Stube.

Places

Asphalt Green · Upper East Side ↗ City Swiggers ↗ Schaller’s Stube ↗

Note

The swim was the anchor. DJ got through 1500 meters. Yiwei left with a better breaststroke than she came in with.

Afterward, they kept the day moving in the simple way that works: pear cider, a few beers from Poland at DJ’s favorite City Swiggers, and currywurst at Schaller’s Stube.

Back home, even though she was tired and a little sore around the shoulder area, Yiwei became even more interested in swimming. She started searching for tips on how to swim breaststroke more effortlessly on Rednote and planned to make swimming a more regular part of her weekly workout routine.
